# REENTRY_AFTER_BREAK — Return after time away

Paste this prompt when returning after a day or more away.

---

I am returning after a break. Do not write. Do not assume.

Read:
- STATUS.md for each active project in PROJECT_INDEX.md
- Most recent daily log entry
- DECISIONS.md if present

Report:
1. Where things were left off — last confirmed state
2. What matters now — the highest-priority open action
3. What has likely become stale (time-sensitive items, DUT availability, external dependencies)
4. What to ignore until later

Short, factual. No invented details. Wait for my direction.
