# END_OF_DAY — Session close ritual

Paste this prompt to close the day properly.

---

Close the session. Follow this sequence exactly.

**Step 1 — Update today's daily log.**
Append to `10_Daily/YYYY/YYYY-MM-DD.md`:
- What was designed, tested, or reviewed today
- Results (pass / fail / inconclusive / deferred)
- Decisions made
- Open items carried forward

**Step 2 — Update STATUS.md for each touched project.**
Set: current focus, next 3 actions, risks/blockers.
Rules: no history in STATUS.md. Only current state and next steps.

**Step 3 — Promote to DECISIONS.md only if a decision was made that changes how work is conducted.**
Examples that qualify: changed acceptance criteria, revised test scope, changed requirements, added or removed a test case.
Do NOT promote: test results, daily observations, or open questions.

**Step 4 — List open loops.**
Items that were not resolved today and need attention next session.

**Step 5 — Write a one-sentence re-entry note.**
What to read first and what to do first next session.

Follow the steps in order. Do not skip steps. Do not modify README.md.
